# 5-Year Week-by-Week Micro Roadmap: Deep Tech → Startup → Executive

> **Principles:**  
> - Focus on **1% improvement every day**  
> - Apply **Atomic Habits loop:** Cue → Craving → Response → Reward  
> - Track weekly tasks with ✅ checkboxes
